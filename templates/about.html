About Us

This application was written using the python framework Django. There are many dependencies in this project, a few of which are listed below:

  • Gunicorn
  • Whitenoise
  • Django Crispy Forms
  • Pillow
  • Django Allauth

Among others. I used docker locally for most of development, orchestrating containers with docker-compose. I am still considering practical uses of this lovely web application, but in the meantime hopefully job recruitment will suffice.

I went to school in Champaign-Urbana studying electrical engineering and computer science. I have always enjoyed the simplicity and the curiosity that computers have brought into my life. To many, both the software and the hardware it runs on is simply magic. But underneath the blanket of computer science and engineering there are many areas which one could focus on. Security is my main concern for a modern society constantly connected the internet, and that is where I focus my efforts.
